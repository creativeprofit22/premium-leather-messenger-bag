# Product Images

## Required Image

Place a product image at: `/public/images/messenger-bag-hero.jpg`

### Image Specifications:
- **Filename:** `messenger-bag-hero.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x1200px (square aspect ratio)
- **Format:** JPEG or WebP
- **Size:** < 200KB (optimized)
- **Alt text:** Already configured in ProductHero component

### Recommended Content:
- High-quality photo of the premium leather messenger bag
- Rich cognac brown leather clearly visible
- Brass hardware prominently displayed
- Professional product photography with good lighting
- Neutral or complementary background

### Temporary Placeholder:
Until you add your product image, the component will display a broken image icon. You can use a temporary placeholder from:
- https://placehold.co/1200x1200/a0522d/ffffff?text=Premium+Leather+Bag
- Or use any product photo from your inventory

### Optimization Tips:
1. Use next/image optimization (already configured)
2. Compress images using TinyPNG or Squoosh
3. Convert to WebP format for better compression
4. Ensure image is sharp and well-lit
